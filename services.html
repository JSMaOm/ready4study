Services

University Application  |  Accommodation  |  City Guide  |  Language Training  |  University Registration  |  Airport Pick-up  |  Residence Permit  |  Paper Translation  |  Scholarship Application  |  Visa Application  |  Entrance Exam Preparation

We are aware how hard and challenging it is to do all the tasks needed to apply and study abroad and we do know that sometimes these tasks are the first reason for the student to quit halfway. Therefore, we are providing our customer students with everything they may need to start their education career abroad. Our services fall under three stages:

Services at Stage 1:

  1. University application

    2. The company interacts directly with the selected university to get information about the documents the student needs to provide. The company will then apply on the behalf of the student for the university. The student will be updated at every step of his application.

  2. English language

    3. Providing the necessary assistance for the student to prepare for the IELTS exam, if required. The company commits to provide the student with previous exams and books to help them ace their test. Should the student needs to take extra English courses, the company, through its relation, will connect the student with the best English learning centers that provide english courses at relatively low prices.

  3. Italian language

    4. Through our concrete relations, we will connect you with the best Italian language centers (when necessary).

  4. Translation and legalization

    5. It is often the case when the university or the embassy asks for documents translation and legalization. We will do that for you!

  5. University scholarship/financial aid

    6. Most universities offer scholarships and financial aid opportunities. We will be responsible to check for such opportunities and submit a request on your behalf.

  6. Entrance exam

    7. The company will apply on the behalf of the student for the entrance exam (if required).

  7. Entrance exam preparation

    8. Our trained staff will help the student prepare for their exam by providing them with previous exams and books that the student cannot have access to otherwise.

  8. Family income statement

    9. The student will be guided by one of our specialists to provide some family statements that are essential for the scholarship. In some cases, the company will provide those documents on behalf of the student.

  9. Health insurance

    10. We will give you a health insurance the embassy will surely ask you for.

  10. Bank account

    11. Instructions on how to start your international account whenever required by the embassy.

  11. Visa

    12. We will make sure you have everything with you when the time comes to apply for your visa. In some situations (not all branches), the company will apply for the visa on the behalf of the student.

Services at Stage 2:

  1. Flight ticket

    2. We will issue you a cheaper-than-ever flight ticket if you ask us so. Just pay for the ticket!

  2. Accommodation

    3. We will not leave our students homeless, we will book them at least three nights in a good accommodation close to the university.

  3. After arrival guidance:

    4. Every student will have their all-inclusive guidance package that will help them from the moment they arrive until they settle down. The student will also be surrounded by other students traveling to the same university so they can make friends before they go.

  4. Contact person

    5. We will also provide the student with a contact number of one of our many agents in Italy should they need urgent help.

Services at Stage 3:

  1. Airport pick up

    2. If you prefer so, one of our agents will be waiting for you at the airport of the destination country, just like a family member waiting for you arrival.

  2. Going to the hotel

    3. Our agent will be your companion from the airport to the hotel

  3. Sim card

    4. You will have your 4G sim card upon arrival.

  4. Transportation weekly pass

    5. You will not worry about getting around while you have this weekly pass.

  5. Going to university

    6. Our agent will be waiting for you downstairs your hotel to accompany you to the university, just in case.

  6. University check-in

    7. You will not get bored in the university queue while our agent is standing with you, should you need any help.

  7. Residence permit

    8. We will fill the residence permit forms for you, you will stay relaxed!

  8. University registration

    9. This is the last step before starting your classes. We will help you with it.

  9. City guide

    10. Our agent will show you the city and give you some great tips

  10. Scholarship application

    11. You will be required to do some legalization of your scholarship-related documents. Our staff will help you do that by setting necessary appointments with the competent offices before your arrival to Italy. This is very tricky because sometimes the scholarship has a strict deadline that the students often miss if they don’t have their appointments already set beforehand.
