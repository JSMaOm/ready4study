  1. The cost of studying and living is very low compared to private universities in the Arab countries

What are the non-academic benefits of studying in Italy?

  1. Italy attracts a large number of foreign students every year, making it a cultural exchange center, which contributes to building the student's experience and broadening the scope of their thoughts

What if I cannot afford the university tuition fees even if they are low?

  1. Through its representatives throughout Italy, the Italian government offers all students, including foreigners, the opportunity to apply for tax reduction and scholarships
  2. Required Documents:
    - Proof of income and property of the student's parents (the company undertakes all procedures for issuing it).
    - Directing a family record.

Does the scholarship cover my living expenses?

Yes, the scholarship can include:

  1. Waiving all tuition fees
  2. Accommodation
  3. Pocket money
  4. Daily meals at discounted prices

  1. Facilitate the procedure to the student, ensuring that the student is not exposed to any case of fraud by external parties.
  2. Accelerate and facilitate the legal procedures for the student to ensure the approval of the university.
  3. Providing the students or parents with the latest updates on foreign higher education institutions that the student wishes to attend, which include recognized academic programs and study systems, the amount of fees and certificates granted, and a rough estimate of the cost of living in the country where the institution is located.
