About Us

Ready4Study is an educational consultancy company based in Italy, Egypt, Lebanon, Jordan and Palestine. The company offers services to help students study abroad in prestigious universities. Of course, we are not going to mention the importance of studying abroad. The cost, the experience, the post-graduation opportunity. Especially for students coming from regions where education is not really supported.

Our objective is to guide students wishing to study abroad during their admission and enrolment procedure. Our job is to direct students during the university selection period and provide them with full information on how to apply and how to prepare the documents they need. The language is one main issue that we will take care of because students usually overlook that they need to have a good language proficiency and at the end their applications.
